Frequently asked questions

How is DocumentGPT different from ChatGPT?

DocumentGPT is built specifically for research and writing. Upload PDFs, chat with multiple documents, get smart highlights, and use Journal mode. ChatGPT can't do any of that.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your documents stay in your workspace. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and are GDPR compliant. We don't train AI models on your data.

Can I really use it for free forever?

Yes! Free plan includes 10 chats/month and 2 documents. No credit card required. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited usage.

How many PDFs can I upload?

Free plan: 2 documents. Premium plan: unlimited documents. Each document can be up to 50MB.
